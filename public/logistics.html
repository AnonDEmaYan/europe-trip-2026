Football — high-profile matches & your dates

Fixtures below are from 2025–26 published schedules (e.g. ESPN · Barcelona, Real Madrid). Kickoffs move for TV — always confirm on the club or La Liga / LFP site before booking travel or tickets.

Fixture When / where Trip fit Time to plan
PSG — Ligue 1 home (Parc des Princes) Check PSG schedule & official tickets for Apr 4–6 while you’re in Paris. Top pick logistically. You have three Paris nights (incl. Easter Sun Apr 5). A Sat / Sun / Mon evening kickoff fits without moving other cities. 5–7 h comfortable: metro to stadium (~30–40 min from central Paris), 60–90 min before kickoff for entry, ~2 h match + stoppage, crowd exit. Add +1–2 h if you want a pre-match dinner or brasserie near 16e / Auteuil.
Atlético Madrid vs FC Barcelona (La Liga) Sat Apr 4, 2026 — listed ~3:00 PM local · Cívitas Metropolitano, Madrid (ESPN match page). Massive game — title-race tier. Your plan has MAD → Paris on Apr 4. Recommendation: book a late evening flight (after ~22:00) or fly Sun Apr 5 morning instead, so Apr 4 is free for the match + metro back to shower/pack. Not compatible with a mid-afternoon departure the same day. 6–8 h block: Atocha area → metro/Light rail to stadium district (~45–60 min), gates early, 90+ minutes play, slow exit. Optional +1 h tapas near Centro before heading out.
FC Barcelona vs Atlético Madrid (La Liga / European week) Wed Apr 8, 2026 — Barcelona “home” (while Spotify Camp Nou work continues, matches are often at Estadi Olímpic Lluís Companys / Montjuïc — verify gate). Poor fit: your US-bound flight is morning Apr 8 (~10:55 BCN). You’d need to move the return to catch an evening kickoff. Skip unless you intentionally extend. If you ever did: same 6–8 h stadium block as above, plus airport buffer the next day.
Real Madrid vs Bayern München (UEFA Champions League) Tue Apr 7, 2026 · Santiago Bernabéu, Madrid — listed ~3:00 PM (1st leg; confirm round). You’re in Barcelona on Apr 7 (Paris → BCN). Not workable without changing flights/hotels to stay in Madrid that night. European night games: treat as 7–9 h if you build a Madrid day around it (pregame + match + late metro).
El Clásico — FC Barcelona vs Real Madrid Sun May 10, 2026 · Barcelona (La Liga). After your trip — best for a future long weekend. Full-day event for most fans: 8 h+ if you include city buzz and post-match.
Our recommendation for this itinerary: (1) PSG at home if there’s a fixture on Apr 5 or 6 evening — lowest friction. (2) If you care more about Spanish giants than Paris, rework Apr 4 travel and aim for Atlético–Barcelona in Madrid — unforgettable, but you must protect a late or next-day hop to Paris. (3) Treat no ticket as OK: a good sports bar / Irish pub with locals is 2–3 h and still memorable.

Easier tickets, big vibe: Rayo Vallecano (Vallecas, Madrid) for underdog energy; RCD Espanyol when they host top sides (cross-city angle vs Barça). Always use official club + named resale partners — avoid random scalper DMs.
