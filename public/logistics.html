Booking checklist

  • Madrid → Paris flight (Apr 4)
  • Paris → Barcelona flight (Apr 7)

  • Sagrada Família timed entry
  • Optional: flamenco Madrid, Ribera wine tour, Champagne day from Paris, Disfrutar / Botín if desired
  • Travel insurance · phone plan · passport copies
  • Delta seats & alerts for DL 194 and your BCN → ATL flight (e.g. DL 195 on many timetables · Apr 8)

Nightlife & sports (optional)

Reggaeton / Latin nights: Paris often has big DJ-led nights at YOYO – Palais de Tokyo — check DICE for your dates. Barcelona: Shôko, Sala Apolo — verify listings close to travel.
Soccer: Big matches (e.g. PSG at Parc des Princes) need tickets from official club / resale rules. Your itinerary has you in Madrid Apr 3 and Paris from Apr 4 — if a match falls on a night you’re in Paris, book early; otherwise enjoy bars or a future trip.

Packing & practical

  • Adapters: Type C/E (same for Spain + France)
  • Layers for late March / early April; comfortable shoes for cobblestones
  • Easter Sunday (Apr 5): some shops closed — check Google Maps hours
  • Inditex: Zara, Massimo Dutti, Bershka often in same strips or malls
