Day by day

Adjust around your actual flight times — especially Apr 7–8 in Barcelona.

  • Tue Mar 31
    Barcelona — arrival
    Land on Delta DL 194 (confirm time on ticket). Easy afternoon: walk, tapas, early night for jet lag.
  • Wed Apr 1
    Barcelona
    Gothic Quarter, Barceloneta sunset. Book a timed Sagrada Família slot if you haven’t yet.
  • Thu Apr 2
    → Madrid
    Morning AVE Barcelona Sants → Madrid Atocha (~2.5–3 h). PM: Retiro or La Latina tapas.
  • Fri Apr 3
    Madrid
    Malasaña / Chueca, market lunch, rooftop drinks. Optional: flamenco tablao or Ribera del Duero wine tour (pre-book).
  • Sat Apr 4
    → Paris
    Flight MAD → CDG/ORY. Evening: Seine stroll, light dinner, settle in.
  • Sun Apr 5
    Paris — Easter
    Many shops closed or shorter hours. Lighter plan: Montmartre, café, or Versailles gardens / Champagne day-trip (Reims · train).
  • Mon Apr 6
    Paris
    Eiffel / Trocadéro, Marais or Latin Quarter wander. Mix in shopping (BHV, Marais boutiques).
  • Tue Apr 7
    → Barcelona
    Flight Paris → BCN. Last tapas, pack, early night before long US flight.
  • Wed Apr 8
    Fly home — Delta DL 095
    BCN → ATL on DL 095. International departure: plan airport arrival, liquids out for security, and Global Entry / Mobile Passport for Atlanta arrival if you use them. Full step-by-step: Guide · Apr 8.
One night in Barcelona at the end: If your Apr 8 flight is very early, consider an airport hotel for Apr 7 night — or move return to Apr 9 for a second buffer night (see logistics).
