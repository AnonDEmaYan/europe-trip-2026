Day by day

Adjust around your actual flight times — especially Apr 7–8 in Barcelona. Target ~10k steps by walking between neighborhoods; mix authentic meals with lighter / spicy nights — see Guide · eat, walk, shop & Explore.

  • Tue Mar 31
    Barcelona — arrival
    Delta DL 194 — published tables (from 8 Jan 2026): 17:50 Eastern ATL → 08:40 BCN next morning (~8h50); Tue / Thu / Sat. Mar 31, 2026 is Tuesday — often dep ATL that evening → BCN morning Apr 1; if your boarding pass shows Mar 31 BCN, follow the ticket. Easy first afternoon: walk, tapas, early night for jet lag.
  • Wed Apr 1
    Barcelona
    Gothic Quarter, Barceloneta sunset. Book a timed Sagrada Família slot if you haven’t yet.
  • Thu Apr 2
    → Madrid
    iryo train 06110 · Barcelona Sants → Madrid (usually Atocha or Chamartín — see ticket; ~2.5–3 h). Details: Logistics · BCN ↔ Madrid trains. PM: Retiro or La Latina tapas.
  • Fri Apr 3
    Madrid
    Malasaña / Chueca, market lunch, rooftop drinks. Optional: flamenco tablao or Ribera del Duero wine tour (pre-book).
  • Sat Apr 4
    → Paris
    Flight MAD → CDG/ORY. Evening: easy Seine stroll (e.g. Île de la Cité → Île Saint-Louis), light dinner, settle in — see Paris scenic.
  • Sun Apr 5
    Paris — Easter
    Many shops closed or shorter hours. Lighter plan: Montmartre (Sacré-Cœur views, café), Versailles gardens, or a Champagne day — Reims / Épernay / Hautvillers from Gare de l’Est (~45 min TGV to Reims). Ideas: scenic Paris · Champagne & nearby.
  • Mon Apr 6
    Paris
    Eiffel / Trocadéro · Seine bridges walk · Marais or Latin Quarter. Mix in shopping (BHV, Marais boutiques). More walks: Explore · Paris scenic.
  • Tue Apr 7
    Paris (last day) → Barcelona
    Spend the day in Paris, then take a high-speed evening train Paris → Barcelona (arrives at Barcelona Sants). Keep the day active, pack light, and plan a quick dinner/early sleep so your Apr 8 flight feels easy.
  • Wed Apr 8
    Fly home — BCN → ATL
    Published DL 195 (from 9 Jan 2026): 10:55 BCN → 15:35 Atlanta (3:35 PM Eastern) (~10h40); Wed / Fri / Sun — Apr 8, 2026 is Wednesday. Confirm flight # on ticket. BCN ~7:55 AM for US check-in if ~10:55 dep. Global Entry / Mobile Passport help at ATL. Guide · Apr 8 · Logistics times.
One night in Barcelona at the end: If your Apr 8 flight is very early, consider an airport hotel for Apr 7 night — or move return to Apr 9 for a second buffer night (see logistics).
